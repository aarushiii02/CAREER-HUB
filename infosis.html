INFOSYS

Infosys Limited is an Indian multinational information technology company that provides business consulting, information technology and outsourcing services. The company is headquartered in Bangalore. Infosys provides software development, maintenance and independent validation services to companies in finance, insurance, manufacturing and other domains.One of its known products is Finacle which is a universal banking solution with various modules for retail and corporate banking.
